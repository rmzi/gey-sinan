# Harari Language: Audio & Video Resources

## Community Media Platforms

### harar.city
**URL**: [harar.city](https://harar.city)

The primary online hub for Harari audio/video content, offering:

#### Video Content
- Harari history documentaries
- Kufut Gar program
- Harari wedding videos
- Health education content
- Harari drama
- Cultural videos
- Religious studies content

#### Audio Collections (Gêy Wiqriyâch)
- Harari poems
- Audio books
- Podcasts
- Music collections

#### Music Categories
- **Harari Musics** - [harar.city/harari-video/6/Harari%20Musics](https://harar.city/harari-video/6/Harari%20Musics)
- **Gêy Faqar** (Love of the City) - [harar.city/harari-music](https://harar.city/harari-music)
- **Songs about Harar** - [harar.city/harari-music/6/Songs%20about%20Harar](https://harar.city/harari-music/6/Songs%20about%20Harar)
- Classical to contemporary albums

### saayharari.com
**URL**: [saayharari.com/hararimusic](https://saayharari.com/hararimusic/)

The Saay Harari Association's media portal featuring:
- Harari cultural songs
- Harari Zikri (religious songs/mawlood)
- Old and new Harari music
- Audio collections

## Radio Programs

### Sây Xaba Radio
- Weekly Harari radio program
- Operated by Australian Saay Harari Association
- Broadcasts in Harari language

## Religious Audio (Zikri/Mawlood)

Available at harar.city and saayharari.com:
- **Harari Zikri** - Traditional religious chanting
- **Harari Mawlood** - Devotional songs
- Old recordings to newly recorded albums
