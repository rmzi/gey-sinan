# Harari Language: Dictionaries & Vocabulary Resources

## Dictionaries

### Wolf Leslau's Etymological Dictionary of Harari
- **Scope**: ~2,200 roots
- **Content**: Etymologies and comparisons with other Ethiopic languages, Semitic languages, and Cushitic
- **Format**: PDF
- **Download**: [everythingharar.com](https://everythingharar.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/LesluHarariDictionary145.pdf)
- **Notes**: The most comprehensive Harari dictionary available

### Glosbe Harari-English Dictionary
- **URL**: [glosbe.com/har/en](https://glosbe.com/har/en)
- **Features**:
  - Phrases with translations
  - Usage examples
  - Pronunciation guides
  - Images
- **Also available**: Translations to/from various languages

### Sewasew
- **URL**: [en.sewasew.com - Harari Language](https://en.sewasew.com/p/harari-language-(%E1%8B%A8%E1%88%90%E1%88%A8%E1%88%AA-%E1%89%8B%E1%8A%95%E1%89%8B))

- **Shadda**: Doubled consonant
- **Sukun**: Vowel absence

## Loanword Categories

### Arabic Loanwords
- Extensively documented by Wolf Leslau
- Special characters for Arabic sounds: ȧ, dh, ḋ, ḣ, gh, ṡ, ṫ, ż

### Cushitic Loanwords
- Unique loanwords found only in Harari (not in other Ethio-Semitic)
- Attributed to Sidama substratum influence

### Modern Loanwords
- English words use P and V (not native to Harari)
